Questionator 3000

A product created for students by students!
Welcome to the Questionator 3000!

This website is an easy yet effective tool that students can use to learn and test themselves on concepts. With minimal effort, students can have various fill in the blank and true/false questions generated on any concept of their choice by following these steps:

  1. Choose a concept to learn or study, and gather material on the concept in the form of text or a pdf.
  2. Click on the appropriate page to upload the material from which the questions will be generated.
  3. Upload the material, and watch as the questions are generated.
  4. Answer each question and check the answers by submitting.

This project was created at HackNYU 2018 by 5 high school students: Sidhu Arakkal, Zachary Colucci, Bryan Yao, Adithya Paramasivam, and Daniel Shulman. We hope that you will enjoy using it. If you have any comments or suggestions, please feel free to use the "contact us" section of the website.
